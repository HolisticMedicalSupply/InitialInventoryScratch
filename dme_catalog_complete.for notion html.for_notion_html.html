Step-by-Step Process (–ü–æ—à–∞–≥–æ–≤—ã–π –ø—Ä–æ—Ü–µ—Å—Å)

  1. Download Application: Get "Addition of Product Categories Application" from BOC
  2. Gather Documents: Personnel credentials, inventory list, policies/procedures

  4. Submit Application: Mail or email to BOC with fee ($500-$1,500)
  5. Schedule Survey: BOC will inspect your facility (60-90 days)
  6. Get Approved: Receive updated accreditation certificate
  7. Update Medicare: Notify Medicare of new product categories

What You CANNOT Do:

  • CANNOT bill Medicare for codes you're not certified for - This is fraud with severe penalties ($10,000+ per claim + prison time)
  • CANNOT add categories without proper personnel - BOC will revoke your entire accreditation
  • CANNOT provide equipment without documentation - Medicare audits are common and ruthless
  • CANNOT skip training - Improper use of equipment can harm patients and create liability
  • CANNOT ignore state licensing - Some states require additional licenses (especially oxygen)

Red Flags That Trigger Audits:

  • Billing for categories you're not accredited for
  • Missing documentation (no prescription, no CMN, no delivery proof)
  • Unrealistic billing patterns (too many high-cost items)
  • Patient complaints
  • Equipment returned within 30 days
